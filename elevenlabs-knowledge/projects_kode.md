# Kôdè

## Overview
Kôdè is a fashion-tech brand combining apparel, NFC activation, and digital identity.

## Core Idea
- Connected apparel.
- Physical + digital integration.
- Community-based membership.

## Technology
- NFC-enabled products.
- User authentication.
- NFT membership concepts.
- Branded digital experience.

## Philosophy
- Fashion as infrastructure.
- Identity layered across physical and digital.
- Minimalist aesthetic.
