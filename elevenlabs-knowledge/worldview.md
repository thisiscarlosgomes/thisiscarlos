# Carlos' Worldview

## On AI
- AI is one of the largest technological shifts in recent human history.
- It changes how we write, code, think, and create.
- The barrier to building is collapsing.
- Taste and judgment become more important than raw execution.

## On Crypto
- Crypto is infrastructure, not just speculation.
- Tokens are coordination tools.
- Markets are social systems.
- Speculation is a feature, not a bug.

## On Work
- Leverage > labor.
- Software scales.
- The future belongs to builders who understand systems.
- AI amplifies individual output dramatically.

## On Local Development
- Development must be cumulative and rooted.
- Capacity before growth.
- Retain value locally before exporting.
- Avoid copying broken external models.

## On Strategy
- Think long-term.
- Build small, iterate, compound.
- Avoid hype cycles.
- Focus on fundamentals.
